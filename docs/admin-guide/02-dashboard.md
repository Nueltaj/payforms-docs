# Section 2: Dashboard Overview

The **Dashboard** is the first screen displayed upon logging in to Payforms. It provides a real-time overview of your institution's payment activity and serves as the central hub for navigating all platform features.

![Figure 7: The Admin/Staff Dashboard](/payforms-docs/assets/images/Picture7.png)

---

## Summary Cards

At the top of the dashboard, four cards display your institution's key financial metrics at a glance:

- **Total Revenue** — The cumulative amount collected across all payment forms, displayed in Naira (₦) alongside the number of successful transactions.
- **Total Transactions** — The total number of payment attempts recorded on the platform, regardless of outcome.
- **Success Rate** — A percentage breakdown of payment outcomes, showing the number of transactions that were paid, partially paid, pending, or failed.
- **Pending Collections** — The total value of payments initiated but not yet completed. Click **Review Pending** to view and act on these records.

---

## Payment Trends

Below the summary cards, the **Payment Trends** chart visualises revenue by status over the trailing seven days. Transactions are categorised as Paid, Pending, and Failed. This chart updates automatically as new payment data is recorded.

![Figure 8: The Payment Trends chart](/payforms-docs/assets/images/Picture8.png)

---

## Recent Activity

The **Recent Activity** panel logs the latest actions taken within your account, including form creation, payment receipts, and status changes. Click **View All** to see the full activity history.

---

## Recent Transactions

The **Recent Transactions** table lists your most recently recorded transactions with the following columns: **Date**, **Reference**, **Form Name**, **Amount**, and **Status**. Click **View All** to access the complete transaction log under the **Payments** section.

---

## Top 5 Performing Forms

The **Top 5 Performing Forms** panel ranks your payment forms by collection rate, helping you identify which forms are most effective at converting payment attempts into successful collections. Click **View All Payforms** to see the full list.

---

## Navigation Sidebar

The left sidebar provides access to all major sections of the platform:

- **Dashboard** — Returns you to this overview screen
- **Forms** — Create and manage your payment forms
- **Payments** — View and filter all transaction records
- **Contacts** — Manage your student and payer directory
- **Audit Logs** — Review a timestamped record of all administrative actions
- **Reports** — Generate and export financial reports
- **Settings** — Configure your institution's account preferences
- **Offline Payments** — Record payments made outside the platform
- **+ Create Form** — Shortcut to launch the Form Builder directly
