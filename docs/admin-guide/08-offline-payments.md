# Section 8: Offline Payments

The **Offline Payments** section allows you to manually record payments made outside the platform, such as cash, bank transfer, POS, or cheque payments. To access it, click **Offline Payments** in the left navigation sidebar or from the **Payments** page.

![Figure 49: The Offline Payments page](/payforms-docs/assets/images/Picture49.png)

---

## Recording an Offline Payment

To add an offline payment, click **+ Add Payment** in the top-right corner of the page, or click **+ Add Payment** in the centre of the screen if no records exist yet. The **Add Offline Payment** form will appear with the following fields:

![Figure 50: The Add Offline Payment form](/payforms-docs/assets/images/Picture50.png)

- **Form** — Select the payment form the transaction is associated with
- **Contact** — Select the contact who made the payment from your existing contacts list
- **Amount** — Enter the amount paid
- **Payment Method** — Select the method used: **Cash**, **Bank Transfer**, **POS**, or **Cheque**
- **External Reference** — Enter any reference number associated with the transaction (e.g. a bank teller number or POS receipt reference)
- **Notes** — Enter any additional information relevant to the payment

Click **Add Payment** to save the record, or **Cancel** to discard.

Once recorded, offline payments will appear in the list on this page and can be searched by reference or contact name. Click **Refresh** to reload the list and reflect any recently added records.
