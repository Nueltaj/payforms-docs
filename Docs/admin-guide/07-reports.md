# Section 7: Reports & Analytics

The **Reports** section provides a detailed breakdown of your institution's financial performance across all payment forms. To access it, click **Reports** in the left navigation sidebar.

You can filter all report data by date range using the date fields in the top-right corner of the page. Click **Export** to download the currently displayed report data as a file.

The Reports section is organised into three tabs: **Overview**, **Form Performance**, and **Group Contributions**.

---

## Overview

The **Overview** tab displays a summary of your institution's payment activity for the selected date range. Four summary cards are shown at the top:

- **Collected** — The total amount successfully received, alongside the number of paid payments
- **Outstanding** — The total amount still pending, alongside the number of pending payments
- **Total Volume** — The combined value of all transaction attempts, regardless of status
- **Failed** — The total value of failed transactions, alongside the number of failed payments

Below the summary cards, two charts provide a visual breakdown of payment activity:

- **Payment Trends** — A line chart showing transaction values over time, categorised by status: Paid, Pending, and Failed
- **Payment Status** — A donut chart showing the proportion of paid versus failed transactions

Further down the page, the **Top Performing Forms** section lists your payment forms ranked by collection performance, showing the number of submissions and amount collected for each.

---

## Form Performance

The **Form Performance** tab provides a breakdown of payment activity at the individual form level, allowing you to compare how each form is performing in terms of submissions and collections.

---

## Group Contributions

The **Group Contributions** tab shows payment activity broken down by contact group, allowing you to track how much each group has contributed to total collections.
